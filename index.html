Embedded Product Design, Development & Certification

Overview

ZM Consultancy (ZMC) Services is focused on embedded product design, development & certification. ZMC is backed by a pool of product designers having extensive experience in developing consumer multimedia, medical parameter monitoring, industrial electronics and payment electronic products.

Our primary focus is to colloborate with our customers in bringing great product ideas into market through a safer and quicker development cycle by using our vast expertise in end to end product development lifecycle. Our motto is to deliver quality product development matching the multivariate needs of cost, time and quality.

With our exposure to various vertical markets, hands on experience on embedded product development through complete life cycle including product certification for regulatory (FCC, CE, IC and Japan PSE) and deep insights into the ecosystem place us at a unique position to accelerate embedded product development. We work very closely with Semiconductor vendors, Electronic Manufacturing Service(EMS) and Certification labs like TuV & UL.

We can be a single window for your product development needs with varying scales and requirements. Using appropriate system architecture(s), design methodologies, development and manufacturing technologies, we will put together right solution matrix to take your design idea to product shipment. Being in this eco-system for long, we understand the need for collaboration and have been partnering with industry leaders to execute such complex projects

Challenges We Address

In embedded space, the challenges are

  • Minimal power consumption
  • Low Memory Footprint
  • High levels of device integration
  • Manage the universal triple project constraints - cost, time and quality
Our domain and technology knowledge, process innovation experience and skills helps us to take any complex turn-key projects from customers and manage them through the life cycle and satisfy stakeholders expectations.

As an example, we have designed, developed and taken to manufacturing Cellular & Satellite based Transceiver for a OEM in US that would run on their next generation Asset Management solution catering to commercial trucks & vehicles working in hazardous locations. We are part of the team for performance tuning, customization, embedded software engineering and development, testing, in-vehicle communications, and diagnostic services for remote management.

In another engagement, we developed a highly integrated payment device (similar to IoT) which can store multiple payment options into a single device. High complexity here is from integration between electronics & enclosure and make it easy to use by normal user
